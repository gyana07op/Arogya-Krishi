About the Project

Expert Guidance for

Arogya Krishi is a symptom-based plant disease diagnosis system built on expert agricultural knowledge and rule-based reasoning. Describe what you observe — yellowing, spots, wilting, or unusual growth — and receive a clear diagnosis with a practical, low-cost treatment plan within seconds.

Designed specifically for Indian crops and small-scale farmers, the system recommends organic solutions like neem oil wherever possible and provides step-by-step prevention tips in accessible language.

Disease Library

Conditions We Detect

Hover each card to explore symptoms and recommended treatments.

🍃

Powdery Mildew

Fungal

Fungal disease appearing as white powdery coating on leaf surfaces. Thrives in warm, humid conditions.

Symptoms
White powdery coatingYellowing leavesStunted growth
💊 Treatment

Apply neem oil spray weekly. Improve air circulation. Avoid overhead watering. Use sulfur-based fungicide if severe.

🪴

Root Rot

Water-Borne

Caused by overwatering or poor drainage, leading to root decay and systemic plant failure.

Symptoms
Wilting despite wateringBrown mushy rootsFoul soil odour
💊 Treatment

Improve drainage, reduce irrigation. Remove infected roots. Apply copper-based fungicide to soil.

🍂

Leaf Spot Disease

Fungal/Bacterial

Circular or irregular dark spots caused by fungal or bacterial pathogens. Spreads rapidly in wet seasons.

Symptoms
Dark spots + yellow haloCoalescing lesionsPremature leaf drop
💊 Treatment

Remove infected leaves. Apply copper bactericide or chlorothalonil. Avoid wetting foliage during irrigation.

🌱

Nitrogen Deficiency

Nutritional

Insufficient nitrogen limits chlorophyll production, causing pale colouring and stunted plant development.

Symptoms
Pale/yellow older leavesStunted growthThin stems
💊 Treatment

Apply urea or ammonium nitrate. Use cow dung manure or compost as organic options. Check soil pH levels.

💧

Underwatering

Abiotic Stress

Drought stress from insufficient irrigation causes cellular dehydration and reduced photosynthesis.

Symptoms
Drooping / wiltingCrispy leaf edgesDry cracked soil
💊 Treatment

Water thoroughly and consistently. Apply mulch to retain moisture. Consider drip irrigation for efficiency.

🐛

Aphid Infestation

Pest

Small sap-sucking insects that colonise new growth, weakening plants and transmitting viruses rapidly.

Symptoms
Curling/distorted leavesSticky honeydewVisible insects
💊 Treatment

Spray neem oil solution. Use strong water jet to dislodge colonies. Introduce ladybirds as natural predators.

🥀

Bacterial Wilt

Bacterial

Soil bacteria invade the vascular system, blocking water transport and causing rapid plant collapse.

Symptoms
Sudden wiltingNo recovery at nightBrown water vessels
💊 Treatment

Remove and destroy infected plants immediately. Rotate crops seasonally. Improve drainage. Solarise soil before replanting.

🌿

Early Blight

Fungal

Alternaria fungus causing concentric ring spots, common in tomato and potato crops during warm wet weather.

Symptoms
Concentric ring spotsBrown lower leavesCollar rot on stems
💊 Treatment

Apply mancozeb or chlorothalonil fungicide. Remove infected lower leaves. Avoid overhead irrigation.

☁️

Downy Mildew

Oomycete

Water-mould pathogen causing grey-purple fuzz on leaf undersides. Prevalent in cool, humid conditions.

Symptoms
Yellow upper patchesGrey fuzz underneathLeaf curl and death
💊 Treatment

Apply copper-based fungicide. Space plants for airflow. Avoid wetting foliage. Use resistant varieties next season.

🦟

Pest Damage

Insect Pest

General tissue damage from chewing insects including caterpillars, beetles, and crop stem borers.

Symptoms
Holes in leavesChewed edgesFrass / droppings
💊 Treatment

Handpick large pests. Apply neem oil or Bt spray. Use pheromone traps for monitoring infestation levels.
